About Me

I am an IT professional and educator with a strong foundation in both technical instruction and academic leadership. I bring a unique blend of hands-on programming expertise, structured documentation skills, and people management experience, developed across education and IT-focused environments.

I create clear, structured, and user-centered technical documentation, including SOPs, system documentation, training materials, onboarding assets, and knowledge bases that support operational efficiency and user adoption. In addition, I contribute to marketing initiatives through content creation, social media management, and brand communication, helping organizations strengthen their online presence, build trust with their audience, and communicate their value effectively.
